Lunar Landers

Blue Moon

Blue Moon lander variants provide low-cost, recurring access to the Moon for both cargo and crew.

Blue Moon’s capability to provide precise and soft landings will enable a sustained human presence on the Moon.

SLD National Team

Under NASA's NextSTEP-2 Appendix P Sustaining Lunar Development (SLD) contract, Blue Origin and its National Team partners will develop and fly the Blue Moon MK2 lunar lander that can make a precision landing anywhere on the Moon’s surface.
